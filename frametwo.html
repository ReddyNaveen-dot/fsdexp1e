College profile

Sri Venkatesa Perumal College of Engineering and Technology (SVPCET) is a private, autonomous institution established in 2001. It is located in Puttur, Chittoor district, Andhra Pradesh. 
Accreditation and Affiliation
Affiliation: Affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur (JNTUA).
Recognition: Approved by AICTE and recognized by the UGC.
Accreditation: Accredited by the NBA and NAAC with a B++ grade. 
Academic Programs
The college offers 30 courses across diploma, undergraduate, and postgraduate levels. 
Undergraduate (B.Tech): Includes specializations in Computer Science (CSE), Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML), Electronics & Communication (ECE), Electrical & Electronics (EEE), Civil, and Mechanical Engineering.
Postgraduate: Offers Master of Business Administration (MBA) and M.Tech programs.
Polytechnic: Offers diploma programs in various engineering streams. 
Admissions (2026)
B.Tech: Admission is primarily based on the AP EAPCET (formerly EAMCET) or JEE Main scores. For 2026 admissions, students should monitor the APSCHE official portal for counseling dates.
Counseling Code: Use the code SVPP during the web-counseling process. 
Placements and Industry Links
Performance: Approximately 80% of students secure placements, with recent median packages reported at INR 5.76 LPA and highest packages up to INR 12 LPA.
Recruiters: Top visiting companies include TCS, Infosys, Tech Mahindra, Wipro, and Bosch.
Training: The college operates a Training and Placement Center (TAP) that provides soft skills and technical training starting from the 3rd year. 
Campus Facilities
Infrastructure: A 25-acre green campus featuring well-equipped labs, a 24/7 library, and Wi-Fi enabled areas.
Amenities: Separate hostels for boys and girls, sports facilities for cricket, volleyball, and indoor games, and an on-campus canteen.
Support: Offers various scholarship options based on academic performance and financial need.
